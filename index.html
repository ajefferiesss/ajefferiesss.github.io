Ten Amusing Facts About Dogs

read Time: About 5 minutes

Dogs are awesome creatures that humas share the planet with and even keep as pets! Here are some awesome facts about dogs to keep you amused.

I love puppies!" - Abigail Jefferies, 2024

1. Dogs are the most popular pet on the planet! 2. They evolved from a now extinct species of wolf dating back to 4500bc 3. They can learn 100+ gestures! What smart pups! 4. Their noses are 40x more sensitive than humans. 5. Many work as assistance dogs to help humans 6. They only sweat from their paws, and have to cool down by panting. 7. They can be right or left-pawed! 8. The Ancient Egyptians saw dogs as god-like! 9. Dogs use body language to express their feelings. 10. Owning a dog is a BIG responsibility! It's just like having a baby; another life to care for and look aftr with lots of love and care.
